About Shxd

Hey guys! Its Shxd. I am a 16 year old artist that is trying to make a change in the world using music. I am trying to rid the world of hatred and all the negativities. My music contains genres like HipHop, Lo-Fi, Bedroom pop, Indie, Ambient and more. I make music from my bedroom using GarageBand and sometimes Bandlab. Most of my music comes from what I have felt and I try to share it with the world so that others can relate to it. I am going to continue my journey of making a change in the world and I will not stop until I haven’t succeeded.
